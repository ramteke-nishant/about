How I Built a Website for Just ₹99 While Searching for an IT Job

Hi, I'm Nishant Ramteke from Nagpur. Born in 1995, I graduated with a degree in Information Technology Engineering. Today, I want to share my journey of building a website for just ₹99 while I was on the hunt for a job as a System Administrator, despite having no prior experience in HTML or website building.

The Beginning of My Journey

As a fresh graduate eager to dive into the IT world, I knew having a personal website would boost my chances of landing a job. However, my budget was tight, and my knowledge of website creation was minimal. Determined to overcome these obstacles, I embarked on a mission to build my own website from scratch.

Step 1: Leveraging Online Resources

My first step was to explore online resources to learn the basics of website building. I stumbled upon chatgpt.com, which became an invaluable guide. The platform's conversational AI helped me understand fundamental concepts and provided step-by-step instructions on creating a website.

Step 2: Setting Up a GitHub Repository

Next, I created a GitHub account. GitHub.com offered free hosting for static websites through GitHub Pages. I learned to set up a repository and push my first HTML files, thanks to the detailed guides and community support available on the site.

Step 3: Finding an Affordable Hosting Service

Although GitHub Pages was a great start, I wanted a more professional look for my website. I discovered Hostinger.com, which offered affordable hosting plans. With a minimal investment of ₹99, I purchased a basic hosting plan and a domain name, giving my website a more polished appearance.

Step 4: Enhancing Website Security

To ensure my website was secure and loaded quickly, I integrated Cloudflare.com. Cloudflare provided a free plan that included a Content Delivery Network (CDN) and SSL certificate, enhancing both the performance and security of my site.

The Final Product

After a few weeks of learning, coding, and integrating various services, my website was finally live. It showcased my resume, projects, and a blog where I shared my IT journey and insights. This not only demonstrated my technical skills but also my ability to learn and adapt quickly-key traits for a System Administrator.

Conclusion

Building a website with no prior experience was a challenging yet rewarding experience. It taught me valuable skills and boosted my confidence as I entered the job market. If you're in a similar situation, don't hesitate to leverage the following resources:

    chatgpt.com
    GitHub.com
    Hostinger.com
    Cloudflare.com

Remember, with determination and the right tools, you can achieve your goals, no matter the starting point.
